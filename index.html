About Becca



VILLA BECCA SEMINYAK is a beautifully designed four bedroom Villa just off Jalan Drupadi in upmarket Seminyak. The Villa offers all the modern facilities for a relaxing holiday enhanced by the large open-air living area combining the lounge and dining overlooking the pool and well-maintained gardens. The fully functional large modern kitchen adjoins the dining area.

The garden and pool area also feature poolside loungers, a gazebo/bale, and a fish pond. Guests can relax poolside, request in-villa spa and massage services, request a Tour, or any other exclusive services by our professional team.
