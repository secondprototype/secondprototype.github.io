About Bella



Enjoy a stylish experience at this centrally – located place. Villa Bella is nestled in Seminyak’s chic and sophisticated neighborhood. It offers a lavish and intimate hideaway, and is newly furnished for a high-end finish.

This modern Villa was built in 2019, has a fully equipped kitchen, hi-speed internet, and dedicated staff to greet you. Private scooter parking. We highly suggest you to bring own scooter due the limited parking access.

This stunning villa invites you to enjoy a luxurious poolside backdrop from the bedroom, as well as a light and closed air living room, all at home amidst a luscious little tropical garden. The fully equipped kitchen and gorgeous table set for two provide everything you need for romantic al fresco dining and lazy breakfasts by the pool.
